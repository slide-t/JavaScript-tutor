Lesson 32: DOM Traversing and Nodes

DOM traversing allows you to navigate the structure of the DOM, moving between parent, child, and sibling elements and nodes.

1. Parent, Child, and Siblings


const item = document.querySelector(".item"); console.log(item.parentElement); console.log(item.children); console.log(item.nextElementSibling); console.log(item.previousElementSibling);

2. Node Properties


console.log(item.nodeName); console.log(item.nodeType); console.log(item.childNodes);
